Workshop Overview

Join us for a comprehensive Calisthenics workshop designed to suit all levels. Whether you’re a beginner or an advanced practitioner, our workshop will provide you with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to elevate your calisthenics practice.

In this workshop, you will learn how to start calisthenics, design effective training programs, understand key skills, prevent injuries, and master essential techniques for warming up and improving muscle and joint function.
